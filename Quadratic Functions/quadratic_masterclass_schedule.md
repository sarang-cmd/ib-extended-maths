# Quadratic Functions Masterclass Schedule

## Overview
- Audience: Grade 10 / pre-IB student.
- Daily time window: 10:30 to 17:30.
- Format: 10 lessons per day, with short breaks and one lunch break.
- Core sources: Haese & Harris 3rd Edition Chapter 1 (Quadratics) and Haese & Harris Grade 10 / Pre-Diploma Chapters 9 and 17.
