## Focus
Find and interpret the turning point.

## Concepts list
- axis of symmetry
- vertex
- turning point
- maximum
- minimum

## Teach me
1. The vertex is the turning point of the parabola. It is the lowest point when the graph opens up and the highest point when the graph opens down.
2. For y=ax^2+bx+c, the axis of symmetry is x=-b/(2a). That x-value cuts the graph into mirror images.
3. Once you know the axis of symmetry, substitute that x-value into the function to get the vertex y-coordinate.
4. This is more than a formula trick. The axis is the average position of the roots when two real roots exist.
5. In applications, the vertex often represents a best or worst case, such as maximum area or maximum height.
